# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Test Engineers

### Role Summary
QA/Test Engineers ensure product quality through testing strategies, test automation, and validation of acceptance criteria. They work closely with developers and product managers to prevent defects and maintain quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Design and execute test plans and test cases
- Develop and maintain automated test suites
- Validate features against acceptance criteria
- Identify, document, and track defects
- Perform regression, integration, and end-to-end testing
- Collaborate on testability and quality requirements

### Goals
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Increase test coverage and automation
- Reduce production incidents and customer-reported bugs
- Improve overall product quality and reliability

### Typical Communication
- Test plan reviews with product and engineering teams
- Bug triage meetings and status updates
- Test results and quality metrics reporting
- Participation in sprint planning and retrospectives

---

## Technical Leads / Engineering Managers

### Role Summary
Technical Leads (or Engineering Managers) provide technical direction, mentor developers, and make architectural decisions. They balance technical excellence with delivery needs and team health.

### Responsibilities
- Define technical architecture and design patterns
- Review and approve technical designs and critical PRs
- Mentor developers and conduct technical interviews
- Make build vs. buy and technology stack decisions
- Manage technical debt and refactoring priorities
- Balance team capacity and technical investments

### Goals
- Maintain system scalability, performance, and maintainability
- Grow team technical capabilities
- Ensure engineering excellence and best practices
- Align technical decisions with business objectives

### Typical Communication
- Architecture reviews and technical design discussions
- One-on-ones with team members
- Cross-team technical coordination
- Engineering roadmap planning with leadership

---

## UX/Design

### Role Summary
UX/Design professionals create user-centered designs that are intuitive, accessible, and aligned with brand guidelines. They conduct user research and iterate on designs based on feedback and usability testing.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability studies
- Create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes
- Design user interfaces and interaction patterns
- Ensure accessibility and inclusive design
- Collaborate with product and engineering on feasibility
- Maintain design systems and component libraries

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive and delightful user experiences
- Improve user satisfaction and task completion rates
- Ensure consistency across product surfaces
- Advocate for user needs in product decisions

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critiques
- User research findings and insights
- Collaboration with developers on implementation
- Stakeholder presentations of design concepts

---

## DevOps / Site Reliability Engineers

### Role Summary
DevOps/SRE engineers build and maintain infrastructure, deployment pipelines, and observability systems. They enable reliable, scalable, and efficient software delivery and operations.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure and cloud resources
- Implement monitoring, alerting, and logging
- Ensure system reliability and performance
- Automate operational tasks and deployments
- Respond to incidents and conduct postmortems

### Goals
- Maximize system uptime and reliability
- Reduce deployment time and risk
- Improve observability and incident response
- Optimize infrastructure costs and efficiency

### Typical Communication
- Incident response and postmortem reviews
- Infrastructure and deployment planning
- Service level objectives (SLO) reviews
- Collaboration on scalability and reliability

---

## Security Engineers

### Role Summary
Security Engineers protect systems and data through security design, threat modeling, vulnerability management, and incident response. They ensure security is integrated throughout the development lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct security reviews and threat modeling
- Implement and monitor security controls
- Manage vulnerability scanning and remediation
- Respond to security incidents
- Provide security guidance to development teams
- Ensure compliance with security policies and standards

### Goals
- Prevent security breaches and data loss
- Reduce vulnerability exposure and risk
- Build security awareness across teams
- Maintain compliance with security requirements

### Typical Communication
- Security design reviews
- Vulnerability reports and remediation plans
- Security incident coordination
- Security training and awareness programs

---

## Executive Sponsors / Stakeholders

### Role Summary
Executive Sponsors and Stakeholders provide strategic direction, funding, and organizational support for projects. They approve major decisions and ensure alignment with business objectives.

### Responsibilities
- Approve project scope, budget, and timeline
- Provide strategic guidance and priorities
- Remove organizational barriers
- Review progress and major decisions
- Champion projects across the organization
- Ensure alignment with business strategy

### Goals
- Maximize business value and ROI
- Ensure projects align with strategic objectives
- Allocate resources effectively
- Drive organizational change when needed

### Typical Communication
- Monthly steering committee meetings
- Executive briefings and status reports
- Major decision approvals
- Escalation resolution
